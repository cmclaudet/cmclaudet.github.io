About Me

I started off by studying physics back in 2012 in London. During that time, I took an interest in game development after doing an internship which exposed me to a bit of Unity, a game engine. I eventually began making mobile games with Unity in my spare time. By day I work full time as a software developer with Unity, and by night I build my own games.

Wisp Child is a story driven adventure game about a young alchemist whose curiosity causes a series of devastating events to happen to the nearby village. It was made with Unity and developed by a team of 3 people: me, Emily Claudet, and Christopher Claudet.

Bugbury is a 2D action game for Android made with Unity. It was a solo project that I developed in 2017.

Astromundus Fight is a 2D fighting game for OSX and Windows made with Love 2D. It is the first game I developed and was a solo project.

Cloud 10 is a simple and fun game where the player has to collect clouds to get to 10. It was the first game I developed with Unity.

Swipe Theft was made during the 2019 Global Game Jam based in Berlin. It was made by a 7 person team with Unity and features a Berliner eager to furnish their new apartment.

Unity 3D Berlin is a meetup group I began organizing in April 2017. The purpose of the meetup is to connect people who are interested in Unity.
